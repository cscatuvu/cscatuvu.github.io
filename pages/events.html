Regular Meetings

Bi-Weekly

Regular meetings are designed for all skill levels. Meetings cover anything related to Cyber Security, from a deep dive of the OSI model, to social engineering from those that practice it in the industry, to how to get started with projects that will build your skill sets.

Saturday Sparring Event

Often Saturdays Before a Competition (See calendar)

Casual event where participants play King of the Hill with a set of VMs. King of the Hill is when a participant tries to exploit vulnerable VMs and then prevent other participants from also getting inside. Jump on Slack or Discord to know when the next meeting is exactly.
